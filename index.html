B Pure Unscented

A Product Extension Proposal for Dollar Tree’s B Pure Line

This case study presents a product extension proposal for an unscented version of Dollar Tree’s B Pure organic cotton pads. The brief includes market analysis, demographic modeling, competitive positioning, consumer demand signals, product recommendations, and campaign strategy.

Project Focus

The proposal identifies an opportunity for Dollar Tree to expand its B Pure line with an accessible, fragrance-free product designed for consumers with sensitivity concerns who are currently underserved at lower price points.

Skills Demonstrated

Consumer insights, market research, demographic analysis, competitive analysis, product strategy, campaign planning, and executive-level business writing.
